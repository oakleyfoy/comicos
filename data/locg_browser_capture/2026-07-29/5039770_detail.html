Pull It

ARE WE THERE YET? ARE WE THERE YET? ARE WE THERE YET? Dr. Jumba thinks that he’s hit upon the perfect quasi-evil plan when he opens an amusement park that features a lovable, blue-furred mascot which he can use to transmit his future schemes into an adoring public’s impressionable brains. But when the original Experiment 626 takes it upon himself to bring Stitch Land’s overly ambitious designs to life, the resulting “attractions” threaten to trap Jumba, Pleakley, and an unlucky focus group of innocent civilians in a never-ending cycle of fun — and danger!

Season pass holders CONNOR RATLIFF and JAMES III line up with artist GRETA XELLA for the ride of a lifetime in Stitch #8 — featuring impressively immersive covers by EDWIN GALMON, JAE LEE & JUNE CHUNG, CIRO CANGIALOSI, and GEORGE KAMBADAIS!
